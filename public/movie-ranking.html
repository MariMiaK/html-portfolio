The Best Movies According to Mari

My top 3 movies of all time.

Top Gun

No explanation needed. Maverick's journey is an absolute must-watch classic.

Chasing Mavericks

True-life story, surfing, the four pillars to success... A heartfelt look at the life of Jay Moriarty, overcoming difficulties to become a beloved legend, friend and husband.

Dead Poets' Society

Another true-life story of the powerful impact a teacher can have.
